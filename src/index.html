Tutorial

Your goal is to fill all spaces with galaxy pieces.

There is a catch, though: all galaxies have to keep their rotational symmetry, cannot be destroyed and cannot overlap!

Drag a galaxy piece to a space to fill or replace that space with the selected galaxy.

You'll notice that the galaxy will keep its symmetry.

If filling a space does not seem to work, pay attention to the opposite end. If the edge of the board or another galaxy's center is in the way, it won't work.

Sometimes it can be useful to lock a galaxy piece in place. If you are confident a space is filled with the correct galaxy, you can click/tap and hold for a bit to lock it.

Click a locked piece to unlock it.

To remove a galaxy piece, you can either drag empty spaces onto it, or drag pieces of the same galaxy onto each other.

The centers of galaxies cannot be removed.

And that's it! Have fun!
