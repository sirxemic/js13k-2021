Instructions:

Fill the empty spaces with galaxies. This can be harder than it sounds, as the galaxies will always keep their rotational symmetry.

To expand a galaxy, just drag one of its tiles onto something else. Dragging it onto another tile of the same galaxy will remove the tiles.
